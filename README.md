## To Run:

`python wsgi.py`

Or if that doesn't work:

### for Windows:

`venv\Scripts\activate`

`set FLASK_APP=main.py`

`flask run`

After you kill your flask app, end the virtual environment with 

`deactivate`

### for Other:

`venv/bin/activate`

`export FLASK_APP=main.py`

`flask run`

After you kill your flask app, end the virtual environment with 

`deactivate`
